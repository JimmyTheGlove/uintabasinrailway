Testimonials

“The Uinta Basin Railway is important to Utah’s economy and rural Utah’s ability to diversify their economic streams. Mineral lease dollars generated from the extraction industry and exporting Utah’s crude to domestic and global markets means more revenue for the Permanent Impact Board, which benefits other infrastructure.” ”Utah Senate President J. Stuart Adams
“The railway represents an important opportunity to improve Utah’s energy infrastructure and environmental stewardship, and to enhance the rural economies of Eastern Utah. I add my voice to the various Utah counties and leaders of my state agencies in support of the project.” ”Utah Governor Gary R. Herbert
“This project is an example of innovative public-private partnership of industry partners, the public sector, and private sector answering America’s growing need for expanded infrastructure. The project could bring increased economic opportunity and job growth to this region. We ask for fair consideration of the Coalition’s application to authorize the Uinta Basin Railway project.” ”Utah Congressional Delegation, U.S. Senator Mitt Romney, U.S. Senator Mike Lee, Member of Congress Rob Bishop, Member of Congress Chris Stewart, Member of Congress John Curtis

News

MEDIA RELEASES:

September 8, 2020

Drexel Hamilton Infrastructure Partners, LP (DHIP) and the state of Utah’s Seven County Infrastructure Coalition (Coalition) have executed an agreement to advance the Uinta Basin Railway—»

Under the agreement, DHIP will purchase the Coalition’s intellectual property funded by Utah’s Community Impact Board, and DHIP will have the exclusive right to develop the railway.

 June 2, 2020

Milestone Reached for the Uinta Basin Railway Project as the Coalition Submits a Petition for Exemption to the Surface Transportation Board »

The Seven County Infrastructure Coalition submitted a Petition for Exemption to the Surface Transportation Board (STB) from the formal evaluation of the transportation merits as they pertain to the Uinta Basin Railway project.

 December 17, 2019

Final Scope of Study Released for Uinta Basin Railway »

The U.S. Surface Transportation Board (STB) released on December 13, 2019, the Final Scope of Study of the Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Uinta Basin Railway.

 September 20, 2019

Seven County Infrastructure Coalition adds preliminary route to Uinta Basin Railway study »

The Seven County Infrastructure Coalition Board voted today to add a new preliminary route to the environmental study being conducted for the Uinta Basin Railway.

 June 19, 2019

NEPA Process Begins on Uinta Basin Railway »

The U.S. Surface Transportation Board kicked-off the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) process today for the Uinta Basin Railway project, sponsored by the Seven County Infrastructure Coalition (Coalition).

 June 14, 2019

Seven County Infrastructure Coalition granted remaining funding for Uinta Basin Railway study »

The Utah Permanent Community Impact Fund Board approved a $21.4 million grant to the Seven County Infrastructure Coalition to fund the remaining planning expenses of the Uinta Basin Railway through the regulatory review and permitting process.

 May 10, 2019

Seven County Infrastructure Coalition selects private partner to finance and build the Uinta Basin Railway »

The Seven County Infrastructure Coalition board voted to select Drexel Hamilton Infrastructure Partners, LP for a public-private partnership to finance and build the proposed Uinta Basin Railway.
